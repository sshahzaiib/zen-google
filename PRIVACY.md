# Privacy Policy — Zen Google

_Last updated: 2026-05-23_

**Zen Google does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.**

- It has **no backend server** and **no analytics**.
- Your preferences — the feature toggles, the AI mode, and the list of sites where you've chosen
  to allow One Tap — are stored using Chrome's built-in extension storage. If you are signed into
  Chrome, they sync through **your own Google account** — they are never sent to us or to any third
  party.
- Site-access permissions are requested **only when you turn a feature on**, and are used **solely**
  to block the Google "Sign in with Google" popup or to remove the AI Overview from search results.
  No page content is ever read, recorded, or transmitted.

Zen Google does not use or transfer any data to third parties, for advertising, for determining
creditworthiness, or for any purpose unrelated to its single function described above.
